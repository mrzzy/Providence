WITHDRAWAL
By withdrawing from Travel Smart Journeys, you will no longer receive any notifications on the programme.
Points will still be awarded for the past trips that have yet to be processed. You may redeem your points for cash rewards anytime, if sufficient, before they expire.
If you had enabled auto points conversion, your points would continue to be automatically converted to cash rewards when there are sufficient points.
